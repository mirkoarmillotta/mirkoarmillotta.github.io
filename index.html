About

Mirko Armillotta is a Postdoctoral Researcher in Statistics at the Department of Mathematics & Statistics and IRIDA Research Centre, University of Cyprus. Prior to his work at the University of Cyprus, he has a visiting researcher at Lancaster University. He has a Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Statistical Sciences from the University of Bologna.


His research interests include: Econometrics, High-Dimensional Time Series Analysis, Integer-valued Processes, Methodological Statistics, Network Time Series, Observation-driven Models, Theory & Methods for Time Series, Volatility forecasting.

Contacts: armillotta.mirko@ucy.ac.cy
